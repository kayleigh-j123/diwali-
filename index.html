Diwali

Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights is one of the most important Hindu festivals. It celebrates the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance.

How is Diwali Celebrated?

People celebrate Diwali by lighting diyas (oil lamps), decorating their homes,creating rangoli patterns, exchanging gifts, sharing sweets, and spending time with family and friends.

Why is Diwali important?

Diwali is a time for prayer, reflection, and celebration. It brings families and communities together and symbolizes hope, prosterity, and new beginnings.
